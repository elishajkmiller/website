About

I'm eliam. I'm a Coordinator at a nonprofit in Missouri that serves people with intellectual and developmental disabilities. I'm also the organization's only developer. The software human services orgs are stuck using is bad. Expensive, locked down, built for billing departments instead of the people doing the work. I'm building Robin to fix that. It's an open-source EHR for human services nonprofits, and it's the most real thing I've made. I run the POW417 website and help with operations. It is a 48-hour human-only filmmaking competition out of Springfield MO. No AI-generated content. Forty-something people show up, make something in two days, and it means something because people made it. Other things I'm into: converting a kei truck to electric with a flow battery I'm building from scratch. Passive security research. The Fediverse. Self-hosting everything I can. Music production in REAPER on Linux Mint. I don't have a CS degree. I learned by building things that mattered to people I know. The work comes first..
